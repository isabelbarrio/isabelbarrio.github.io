Lemon pasta

My personal website Back to list of recipes
Ingredients:

1 lemon, juiced.
Zest of a lemon.
5 tablespoons of butter.
A couple of shallots.
A bunch of parmesan cheese to top it.
Extra virgin olive oil.
A few leaves of basil.
Spaghetti (100-150g).
Salt.
A little bit of mint.


Original recipe

  • Melt the butter on a pan, and a bit of olive oil in low heat. Then add the shallots. When they are translucent, add two leaves of basil and some slices of lemon zest.



  • Boil water, salt it, and cook the pasta.




  • Add a little bit of pasta water in the sauce an put in the pasta when cooked. Mix well.

  • Add the lemon juice, don't let it cook too much. Now add the mint, a bit more of chopped basil, and a bit more grated lemon zest. Finally add Parmesan and let it melt so the pasta is creamy.
